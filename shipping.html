Shipping Policy

Effective as of March, 2025

Shipping Areas

Currently, Artystik App ships products within India. We are working on expanding our shipping services internationally.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Please note that processing times may vary during high demand periods or holidays.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

We use reliable courier partners to ensure timely delivery of your artwork. Delivery times may vary depending on your location, but typically, orders are delivered within 7-10 business days after processing.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the weight and destination of the package. The applicable charges will be displayed at the time of checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email, allowing you to track the progress of your delivery.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at artystik.app@gmail.com with your order details so we can resolve the issue.
